# 🧵 True Cost of Fast Fashion

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes the hidden environmental and social costs of fast fashion. While fast fashion offers affordable and trendy clothing, it has significant impacts on the environment and labor conditions.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- To analyze the environmental impact of fast fashion
- To study factors like water usage, carbon emissions, and waste
- To understand social issues such as labor conditions and wages
- To provide insights for more sustainable practices

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Excel (Data Cleaning & Analysis)
- Tableau (Data Visualization)

---

## 📊 Dataset
- Clothing production data
- Water consumption statistics
- Carbon emission data
- Labor and wage reports

*(Datasets sourced from public platforms like Kaggle)*

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Fast fashion contributes to high carbon emissions and environmental pollution  
- Large amounts of water are used in textile production  
- Overproduction leads to significant textile waste  
- Workers in fast fashion industries often face low wages and poor working conditions  

---

## 📉 Visualizations
- Carbon emissions by production volume  
- Water usage in textile manufacturing  
- Waste generated by fast fashion industry  
- Trend analysis of clothing consumption  
---

## ✅ Conclusion
Fast fashion has serious environmental and social consequences. The project suggests adopting sustainable practices such as:
- Reducing overconsumption  
- Supporting ethical brands  
- Promoting recycling and reuse
